In Viewer Mode (press V to enter)

  • Move the scroll wheel to switch between active sharks.
  • Hold the left mouse button and drag to translate the active shark.
  • Hold the middle mouse button and drag to scale the active shark.
  • Hold the right mouse button and drag to rotate the active shark.

In Camera Mode (press C to enter)

  • Move the scroll wheel to switch between active sharks.
  • Hold the left mouse button and drag to pan the camera.
  • Hold the middle mouse button and drag to zoom the camera.
  • Press P to "examine".
  • Press Q to "look around".
